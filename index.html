UIUC Class Schedule to Google Calendar

Instructions:

  1. Get your schedule from courses.illinois.edu
  2. Log in then go to my.Courses on the right
  3. Copy and paste your schedule in the box below
  4. Enter the start and end dates of your class schedule
  5. Authorize your Google account to add the schedule to your calendar
