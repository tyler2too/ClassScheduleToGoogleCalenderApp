UIC Class Schedule to Google Calendar

Instructions:

  1. Log into myUIC (link dosent work for some reason but its just the UIC portal)
  2. Go to 'Student' tab
  3. Scroll down and locate schedule
  4. Copy and paste schedule down below
  5. Enter start and end dates for the semester (add 1 extra day to the end)
  6. Click 'Submit', log in to Google, and allow access to the calendar
  7. If there is a WARNING, click on 'Advanced' and select "Go to tyler2too.github.io(unsafe)"
