UIC Class Schedule to Google Calendar

Instructions:

  1. Get your schedule from myUIC (link dosent work for some reason but its just the UIC portal)
  2. Log in
  3. Then go to Student tab
  4. Scroll towards the bottom of the screen and locate ur schedule
  5. Copy and paste your schedule in the box below
  6. Enter the start and end dates of your class schedule
  7. Submit and login to your Google account to add the schedule to your calendar
  8. If there is a warning, click advanced and click "Go to tyler2too.github.io(unsafe)"
